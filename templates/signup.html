Nouri (& Local Foodbank) Signup Form

This form is for low income families with school aged, unborn, and disabled who are in need of food assistance. By submitting this form you agree:

  • That your household includes school aged, disabled or unborn children.
  • That you and your family struggle with providing nutritious meals for your children due to financial struggles and gaps in welfare or other services for low income families.
  • To share contact and delivery information we need to deliver the food to you and your family (address to deliver food to, and sign up to our Slack app to send you notifications about your delivery.
  • To allow us to share your information with local food banks we work with to help them with their compliance and data collection.
  • To provide feedback to help us make improvements to our service when we send them out. We need to know what is working and what isn't so that we can know what we can do better.
  • To acknowledge that Nouri is a non profit run by volunteers and we work with other non profits and the generosity of local businesses to get food to you. We do not have big funding at this time. Currently, our volunteers take time out of their day and funds from their own pockets to help us validate and improve an idea.
  • That you or someone you trust will be available to receive delivery on the days you accept deliveries or let us know if we are able to safely leave perishable food in an insulated bag you leave by your door. We will try to provide insulated bags for you when we have ones available.
  • That Nouri and our volunteers will try our best to accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions but we also need your help in identifying what items we source are good or not. Because of this, per delivery days, we will be sourcing items that will accommodate everyone on our list as much as possible and to the best of our abilities and resources.
  • That whenever you don't need our service, you will let us know so that we can direct resources to other families in need.
  • This service is only currently going to be available in the following cities: Redmond, Kirkland, Bellevue, Woodinville, and Duvall BUT we are looking to expand to neighboring cities once we have enough signups there.
  • That you sign up and communicate with us through our community app (Slack) which is available for most mobile phones and also as a desktop website. This app helps us with our logistics and communications. It allows our logistics team to message you without needing your phone number or email.

THE PLAN:

  • We send you a notice by our Slack App asking if you're able to accept a delivery for the week. If you have a smart phone, please make sure your operating system is up-to-date. If you don't have a smart phone, the app is available as desktop apps or in the browser on a computer.
  • When we are able to add you to our delivery list you get a message from us, you reply YES or NO within the deadline given (currently Monday’s or Wednesday’s 5pm PST).
  • We source and deliver fresh, perishable food every other week or twice a month depending on shopper/driver availability.
  • Give us your feedback and to make sure to keep up with our announcements.

After the sign up, you should be taken to a page with a link to sign up for our Slack Workspace. If you have any issues or trouble, please let us know. Send us an email at hello@teamnouri.org and we will do our best to help you.
